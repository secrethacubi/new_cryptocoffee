Section 01

Project Overview

Crypto & Coffee is a modern Web3 culture and conversation platform centered around meaningful discussions with builders, thinkers, creators, and innovators. The website serves as the brand's digital home — a content hub, audience funnel, sponsor showcase, and community gateway.

What This Website Must Achieve

Primary Goal
Content Hub

Central destination for all episodes, clips, articles, and media — driving audience from social platforms to owned property.

Growth Engine
Audience Funnel

Convert visitors into subscribers (newsletter, YouTube, socials). Every page funnels toward a subscription action.

Revenue
Sponsor Showcase

Professional presentation for potential sponsors. A dedicated partnership page that sells credibility and reach.

Central Hub
Links & Discovery

A branded links page replacing Linktree — every social, platform, and CTA in one place. The one link you share everywhere.

Brand Positioning (For Developer Context)

This is NOT just a crypto podcast site. It is a premium future-culture media platform. The website must feel like a meaningful conversation you'd genuinely enjoy over coffee — warm, cinematic, modern, approachable, intelligent, and emotionally human. Think "Diary of a CEO meets Bankless meets The Verge" — not a generic podcast landing page.

The Site Should Feel
  • Premium & cinematic — like a media company
  • Warm & approachable — coffee shop energy
  • Modern & future-oriented — Web3 native
  • Emotionally human — real conversations, real people
  • Clean, fast, and intentional
The Site Must NOT Feel
  • Generic podcast template (Podpage, Anchor default)
  • Loud crypto aesthetics (neon, laser eyes, moon rockets)
  • Cheap or unfinished (placeholder content, broken layouts)
  • Overly corporate or stiff
  • Engagement-farming / clickbait energy

Target Audience

Young builders, entrepreneurs, founders, operators, creators, investors, tech-forward individuals, AI and Web3 enthusiasts, and growth-minded people — approximately ages 15–40. Internet-native, ambitious, curious, optimistic, and future-oriented.

Typography Rules: Headlines use serif (warm, editorial). Body uses clean sans-serif (modern, readable). Never mix more than these three families. Headings should have tight letter-spacing (-0.02em on h1). Body text max-width should be 680px for comfortable reading.

Visual Effects & Motion

  • Scroll-triggered fade-in animations (staggered, bottom-up) for cards and sections
  • Subtle parallax on hero images/backgrounds (not aggressive)
  • Hover state on cards: slight lift (translateY -2px) + border color change + subtle glow
  • Page transitions: smooth crossfade between routes (Framer Motion AnimatePresence)
  • Loading states: skeleton screens with espresso-tinted shimmer effect
  • Cursor: default (no custom cursor — keep it accessible)
  • Background texture: subtle noise/grain overlay at 2–4% opacity for depth
  • Gradient accents: use radial gradients with espresso/gold at very low opacity for ambient warmth

Section 19

Content & Copy Guide

All copy on the website should follow the established brand voice. This guide ensures consistency even when multiple people are writing.

Brand Voice Pillars (For All Website Copy)

Pillar 1
Warm but Credible

Write like a smart friend who works in the industry — not a textbook, not a hype account. Contractions are fine. Jargon only when the audience already knows it.

Pillar 2
Optimistic but Grounded

We believe in the future but we don't hype. No moon emojis, no "wagmi," no "to the moon." Excitement comes through specificity, not volume.

Pillar 3
Human-First

Always lead with people, not technology. "Meet the builder behind..." not "Introducing the protocol that..." Stories over specs.

Pillar 4
Concise but Rich

Every sentence should earn its place. No filler. But the site should feel full and complete — not sparse or under-built.

Copy Do's and Don'ts

Write Like This
  • "A conversation about building what's next"
  • "Real talk with the people shaping Web3"
  • "Every week, Phil sits down with..."
  • "Join 10K+ builders who tune in weekly"
Never Write Like This
  • "BULLISH on this alpha! NFA DYOR 🚀🔥"
  • "The #1 crypto podcast in the universe!!!"
  • "Subscribe NOW before it's too late!!!"
  • "Welcome to our innovative blockchain ecosystem platform"
